About us

Legit Transit Limited is a leading travel agency based in Cameroon with a share capital of more than 50 Million FCFA, catering to both domestic and international travel needs. Originally founded by Krensky Akio and Larrini Abeh, with a co-founder  Felix Enow.

With a strong commitment to excellence and a passion for delivering exceptional travel experiences, Legit Transit Limited has established itself as the premier travel agency in Cameroon.


At Legit Transit Limited, we believe that travel has the power to enrich lives, broaden horizons, and create lasting memories. Our mission is to provide our clients with seamless and unforgettable travel experiences that exceed their expectations
